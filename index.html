Process

How StudoCorp Works

A simple four-step process connecting schools, buses, students, and parents in real time.

01

School Registers

Your school signs up and shares student lists. We onboard your transport partners and set up routes.

02

Student is Assigned

Each student is linked to a bus route. Parents receive login access to track their child's journey.

03

Travel Begins

At break time, students board their assigned bus. Live status updates are sent to parents and school admin.

04

Safe Arrival Confirmed

When a student reaches their destination, a confirmation is logged and the parent is notified instantly.
