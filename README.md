## About

StudoCorp (formerly **Kidtico Travel Linkers LLC**) is a Nairobi-based transport management platform that empowers students, high schools, and parents to track school travel in real time during term breaks.

Parents know which bus took their child, who is with them, what time they boarded, and when they arrived safely — all at no extra charge.

---

## Features

- 📡 **Real-time bus tracking** — live status for every student on every route
- 🔔 **Parent notifications** — instant alerts on boarding, transit, and arrival
- 📋 **Admin dashboard** — full visibility for school administrators
- 💳 **Flexible fare payments** — pay in full or in installments, anytime
- 📱 **Fully responsive** — works seamlessly on mobile, tablet, and desktop
